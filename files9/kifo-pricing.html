Common questions

What happens to the memorial if I cancel? ▾
Your memorial stays online permanently on every plan including Free. Cancelling Premium simply means you lose access to premium features. Your photos, tributes, and memories are never deleted.
Can I get a refund? ▾
Yes. Every paid plan comes with a full 30-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked. Email us within 30 days and we'll refund you immediately.
Is my payment information secure? ▾
All payments are processed by Stripe — the same infrastructure used by Amazon and Google. We never store your card number. Kifo only sees the last 4 digits and expiry date.
What does "Lifetime" actually mean? ▾
You pay once and all premium features remain active for as long as Kifo exists. The memorial can also be transferred to a family member at any time.
Do you show ads on memorials? ▾
Never. Kifo is completely ad-free on every plan. Memorial pages are sacred spaces — we will never place advertisements on them.
